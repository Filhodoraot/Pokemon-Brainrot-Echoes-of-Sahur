# Pokemon Brainrot: Echoes of Sahur - Advanced Game Roadmap

This document keeps the advanced story work organized without breaking the playable build.

## Current implemented story coverage

The playtest currently covers the early-to-mid story through:

1. Training Center 01
2. Test Zone 01
3. Safe Zone 01
4. Zone Green 01
5. License D City
6. Moon Noise Cave
7. License C City
8. Sound Port
9. Memory Town
10. Memory Tower

These phases introduce Roan, Kiunin, Dr. Avelar, COGNIA, the Sahur signal, Luna hints, 67 echoes, and the first major memory corruption arc.

## Rules that must stay locked

- Legendary Brainrots do not evolve.
- Legendary Brainrots do not appear as normal gym or route encounters.
- Tung Tung Sahur is a legendary myth form, not a Geodude-line evolution.
- Starters begin in first form only.
- The first Kiunin battle must happen before Route 1/Brock progression.
- Playtest builds must stay green before adding new heavy phases to the workflow.

## Next advanced phases

### Phase 7 - Skibidy Corruption City

Map target: Celadon City and Rocket Hideout.

Story goal:

- Celadon becomes Skibidy Corruption City.
- The Game Corner becomes a corrupted signal arcade.
- Rocket Hideout becomes a COGNIA/Echo Mask backdoor.
- The Skibidy signal is treated as low-quality repeating noise that weakens focus.
- 67 appears in mirrors, drains, and bathroom tiles.
- Luna is heard behind water/static.

Tone:

Not just toilet jokes. The area should feel funny at the surface, but wrong underneath.

### Phase 8 - Red Garden / Strawberry Elefanto

Map target: Fuchsia / Safari style area or custom garden zone.

Story goal:

- Kiunin's Cognitive Lock starts breaking.
- Dr. Renan's lies are exposed.
- Strawberry Elefanto appears as a major legendary/boss presence.
- Renan sacrifices himself to protect Kiunin.

Key line:

"I thought lies could protect you. I was wrong. Live in the truth, Kiunin."

### Phase 9 - Cognia Core City

Map target: Saffron City / Silph Co.

Story goal:

- Dr. Dante Vilar steps forward as main human villain.
- COGNIA is revealed as more than a research group.
- Dante explains his ideology: less thought means less pain.
- Roan and Kiunin become an actual duo.

### Phase 10 - Experiment Island

Map target: Cinnabar Island.

Story goal:

- Old hospital records are recovered.
- Luna evidence becomes undeniable.
- Brainrot creation experiments are shown.
- A failed Sahur vessel can appear as a boss.

### Phase 11 - Area Null / License A

Map target: late-game restricted zone.

Story goal:

- Normal Kanto rules stop working.
- Map logic, memory, NPC identity, and names become unstable.
- The player earns License A.

### Phase 12 - True 67

Story goal:

- 67 becomes more than a meme number.
- It is revealed as the first possession pattern after the hospital event.
- A policeman/first witness was forced to repeat the signal.
- Children copied it, causing a secondary outbreak.

### Phase 13 - Sahur Core

Story goal:

- Roan enters the mental signal system behind Kanto.
- Kiunin helps resist the noise.
- Dante tries to open the path to Tung Tung God Form.

### Phase 14 - Sahur Realm / Final

Story goal:

- Luna is found inside the erased memory space.
- Four altars unlock the final route.
- Tung Tung God Form appears.
- Final battle: Supreme Tung Tung.

## Build safety rule
